Dengue Fever

Prevent mosquito breeding, use mosquito repellent

Symptoms:

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Pain behind eyes
  • Joint/muscle pain

Prevention Tips:

  • Use mosquito nets while sleeping
  • Clean stagnant water around house
  • Wear full-sleeve clothes
  • Seek immediate medical help if fever persists

Common diseases and prevention tips

Diarrhea

Maintain hygiene, drink safe water

Symptoms:

  • Loose stools
  • Stomach pain
  • Dehydration
  • Weakness

Prevention Tips:

  • Drink only boiled or filtered water
  • Wash hands with soap before eating
  • Avoid street food and raw vegetables
  • Take homemade ORS (salt-sugar solution)

Cholera

Safe drinking water, proper sanitation

Symptoms:

  • Severe dehydration
  • Watery diarrhea
  • Muscle cramps

Prevention Tips:

  • Drink properly boiled water
  • Boil drinking water for 10 minutes
  • Eat thoroughly cooked food
  • Visit health center if symptoms worsen

Typhoid

Vaccination, safe food and water

Symptoms:

  • Prolonged fever
  • Weakness
  • Stomach pain
  • Loss of appetite

Prevention Tips:

  • Complete full course of antibiotics
  • Eat light, easily digestible food
  • Rest adequately
  • Drink boiled water only

Common diseases and prevention tips

Pneumonia

Keep warm, get vaccinated

Symptoms:

  • Cough with mucus
  • Chest pain
  • Fever
  • Difficulty breathing

Prevention Tips:

  • Keep children and elderly warm
  • Get pneumonia vaccination
  • Avoid cold water baths
  • Seek medical help if breathing difficulty occurs

Cold/Flu

Maintain hygiene, avoid close contact

Symptoms:

  • Runny nose
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Body ache

Prevention Tips:

  • Drink warm water and herbal tea
  • Gargle with salt water
  • Rest adequately
  • Cover mouth while coughing/sneezing
